During the past five years at the University of Cincinnati I have grown tremendously as a professional and as an individual. If I were starting over again, I would have taken more software focused classes closer to the beginning of my degree. My learning and growth in the co-op program was was fulfilling because it gave me many real world industry experience which has made me a more confident software engineer earlier in my career.
